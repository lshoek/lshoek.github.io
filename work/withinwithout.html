Within Without I & II

Nick Verstand, 2021

The Within Without software is a custom NAP application that animates, controls and pre-visualizes about 9000 LEDs embedded in a massive ring at 1000Hz. It was commissioned for a special performance lead by Nick Verstand in Royal Theater Carré. The performance was such a success, that the ring continued to appear at the VMA's and Awakenings ADE.

I wrote a technical blog post about the sofware development of Within Without:
